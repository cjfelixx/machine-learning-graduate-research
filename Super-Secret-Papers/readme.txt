This toolbox allows to simulate results depicted in

C. Richard, J. C. M. Bermudez, and P. Honeine, "Online prediction of
time series data with kernels," IEEE Transactions on Signal Processing, vol. 57, no. 3, pp. 1058-1067, 2009.

execA.m - Simulation A in the above-referenced paper (section V.A)

execB.m - Simulation B in the above-referenced paper (section V.B)
